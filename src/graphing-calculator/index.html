Help

    In the equation input, put a mathematical equation and press submit. Example equations:

    • x^2 + 2x + 1
    • 3sin(1.5x)
    • (x+1)/(x-1)

    To adjust the range and domain of the graph, drag using the left mouse button, to zoom in/out use the scroll wheel. You can also use the xmin, xmax, ymin, and ymax values and press "Update graph" to apply the changes.

    • xmin is the x value on the left side of the graph.
    • xmax is the x value on the right side of the graph.
    • ymin is the y value on the bottom of the graph.
    • ymax is the y value on the top of the graph.

    Click anywhere on the graph to make a point on the closest equation.
